Our Original Dataset

We received around 50.000 car images from WENN, with annotations (bounding boxes) for the car damages. The car damages are however not classified yet.
Our team therefore built an annotation tool to manually classify each car damage into 4 possible classes: Scratch, Dent, Rim and other. The tool assigns images randomly, and each image is assigned to two people. If the two people annotated an image differently, a third annotator is involved to resolve the conflict.
